The Turkish city of Alanya

We will share with you the things you can do and see in Alanya. Because this city is filled with good energy, tasty food, and acient fortifications.

We will start with Alanya Castle, the Red Tower builtwith stones and bricks, the Aspendos Theater, and the Damalatas Cave.

Galata Tower Istanbul

The most well-known city in Turkey is undoubtly Istanbul. Although it's not the capital, it's the tourist and cutural centre os the country.

A key recommendation is to enjoy the sunset from a boat on the Bosphorus or from Galata Tower. And if you want to buy local products, walk through the Grand Bazaar, where you'll find antiques, carpets, and jewlery of the finest quality.


Canakkle

Canakkle is a small Turkish city, where you'll witness the scene of the First World War on the Gallipoli Peninsula, full of memorials and cemeteries of fallen soldiers.

In the center of the city, you'll find the Cimenlik Castle, the Mirro Bazaar, and of course, the Ruins of Troy.

In Troy, you'll find archaeological remains showing stages of the "9 Trojans" and a gigantic wooden horse as a symbol of the city.
